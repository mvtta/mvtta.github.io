Information, depending on the field of study, takes different meanings or encompasses a different set of attributes.

Is it possible to formulate a generalized definition for Information? The question, although simple at first glance, has by design the term possibility, an open invitation to ask:

  • Is information part of the origin of life equation?
  • Is information part of the natural laws?
  • Is information physical or philosophic?
  • Is it possible to have one definition of Information, because it is part of the natural world and can be generalized or is it an artifact of language, are language and culture part of the natural laws that govern the ?

What if it is not possible, because Information is just an axis on a dimensional space to which we don’t have access? Is information metamorphic, does it have a continuum, or does it belong in a spectrum?

There is no ambition in my words to dissect all the lexicons and references to provide multiple transcripts of publications. This exploration of Information is an exercise in synergetic analysis, guided by a non formal intuition of an underlying broader symbolic relationship.

Why is it usefull to question something so robust as the current definitions and the distinction between Information Theories?

If we are not the true destination of infomration, what else is in the realm of and qualifies an observer? Even if nothing of value comes out of this, the model will not suffer when questioned, would only solidify it’s significace.
